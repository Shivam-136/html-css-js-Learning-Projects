In 1983, a boy named Will Byers disappears in the town of Hawkins. His friends search for him and meet a mysterious girl called Eleven, who has special powers. Wills mother and the police discover that a secret government lab has opened a gate to a dark world called the Upside Down. A monster from this world is behind the disappearances. With Eleven’s help, Will is rescued, but the Upside Down is still not fully gone.
